About Favio Inker

Indie Dance Pioneer

Favio Inker is a Buenos Aires-born DJ and producer now based in Dublin, Ireland. With a deep passion for indie dance music, he has established himself as a prominent figure in the electronic music scene.

Slow Cycle Records

Founder and head of Slow Cycle Records, Favio curates innovative electronic music that pushes the boundaries of indie dance. The label has become a hub for forward-thinking artists and distinctive soundscapes.

Sound & Style

Specializing in indie dance, his productions and DJ sets blend intricate melodies with driving rhythms, creating an immersive experience on the dance floor. Whether through original tracks or remixes, Favio's work reflects his commitment to quality and artistic expression.
